How You Can Help  

Operating a FIRST robotics team requires a variety of resources:  

Tools - building a robot requires numerous tools and instruments.  

Materials - once the design is finalized, materials and fabrications services are required for building the robot.  

Food - students attend meetings regularly throughout the robot BUILD season  

Funding - registration fees, travel expenses, tools, materials, and fabrication services are just a few of the numerous costs that must be covered.  

Sponsorship Levels

 To recognize the support of our sponsors, 2702 Rebels have developed a multi-tiered sponsorship program for monetary and in-kind sponsorships. All sponsors will receive recognition on our team webpage. Additional opportunities exist for companies to have their corporate logo included on the webpage and the robot. Our top level sponsors will be included as part of our team name at events.
